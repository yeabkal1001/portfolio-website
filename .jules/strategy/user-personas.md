# User Personas

## Persona 1: Sarah
- **Role**: Senior Tech Recruiter at a Global Tech Firm
- **Age**: 30-45
- **Goals**: Quickly identify candidates with strong React/Next.js skills and a high eye for detail. Needs to see "proof of work" through interactive projects.
- **Pain Points**: Sifting through dozens of generic, template-based portfolios. Lack of clear technical metadata on portfolio sites.
- **Tech Savvy**: Intermediate
- **Device**: Desktop (primary for work), Mobile (secondary for quick screening)

## Persona 2: Marcus
- **Role**: Creative Director at a Boutique Design Agency
- **Age**: 28-40
- **Goals**: Find a developer who can "speak design." Looking for someone who can implement complex GSAP animations and 3D splines without compromising performance.
- **Pain Points**: Developers who can code but can't replicate design nuances. Sites that look good but feel "heavy" or laggy.
- **Tech Savvy**: Advanced (UI/UX focused)
- **Device**: Desktop (high-res display)

## Persona 3: Henok
- **Role**: Tech Startup Founder (Addis Ababa)
- **Age**: 25-35
- **Goals**: Build a world-class digital presence for a local startup. Wants a developer who understands the local context but can deliver international quality.
- **Pain Points**: Difficulty finding local talent that masters modern stacks like Next.js and Framer Motion.
- **Tech Savvy**: Intermediate/Advanced
- **Device**: Mobile (predominant in local market), Desktop
