## Mood Keywords
- **Kinetic**: Everything feels alive, responding to scroll and interaction with 60fps precision.
- **Sleek**: Deep blacks and high-contrast accents create a premium, high-tech feel.
- **Digital**: Embraces its digital nature through monospace type, glitch effects, and grid systems.
- **Ambitious**: Pushes the boundaries of standard portfolio layouts with an experimental Bento structure.

## Visual References
- **Inspiration**: Awwwards-winning portfolios (e.g., Active Theory, Locomotive), high-end tech landing pages, and the vibrant neon/industrial contrast found in modern urban Addis Ababa.
- **Differentiation**: Moves away from safe "minimalist white" templates to embrace a bold, high-contrast dark aesthetic that highlights technical proficiency.

## Design Principles
1. **Motion as Meaning**: Animations are not just "eye candy"; they guide the user's eye and provide feedback on technical depth.
2. **Contextual Contrast**: The "clash" of Wide Sans and Mono typography creates a unique hierarchy between high-level storytelling and technical metadata.
3. **Structured Chaos**: The Bento grid provides a rigid structure that allows for experimental content within individual cells.

## Imagery Style
- **Photography**: High-contrast, desaturated images with neon green color grades or overlays.
- **Illustrations**: Minimalist 3D elements (Blender/Spline) that respond to scroll; subtle glitch effects.
- **Icons**: Outlined, technical icons that look like blueprint elements or UI components.

## Animation Philosophy
- **Motion style**: Fluid and momentum-based using Lenis Scroll; GSAP for complex timelines.
- **Micro-interactions**: Snappy, playful feedback (e.g., the pixel art avatar glitching on hover).
- **Page transitions**: Seamless, high-performance transitions that maintain the "digital" theme (e.g., grid-based reveals).
